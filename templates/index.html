About

Streamlix

Streamlix is an open-source video downloader designed to make downloading online content simple, fast, and accessible for everyone. It allows users to download videos from popular platforms like Instagram and YouTube completely free of cost, without any hidden limitations.

Features

  • Free and open-source
  • Instagram and YouTube downloads
  • Multiple resolution options (including HD)
  • Audio-only download support
  • Fast and easy-to-use interface
